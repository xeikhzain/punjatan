About Punjatan.com

Welcome to the world of Punjatan.com, a proud division of Punjatan Tabarrukat Centre (PTC), and a distinguished establishment which exists from 1988. We Are The Country's Only Premiere Brand offering Elegant designs and an extensive variety of products accessible online for our esteemed customers.

PTC boasts a rich history steeped in the art of crafting quality products. Over the course of several decades, we have successfully undertaken numerous projects for diverse organizations, communities, and Islamic missions. Our commitment to delivering products of unparalleled workmanship, meticulous attention to detail, and unwavering dedication has gathered widespread appreciation.

We extend heartfelt gratitude to all our customers, whose trust and belief in us have fueled our journey towards providing the very best in quality products. Your continued support inspires us to reach new heights and serve you with excellence. At Punjatan.com, we remain devoted to upholding our legacy of commitment, craftsmanship, and customer satisfaction. Thank you for choosing us as your preferred destination for premium Islamic products.
